<p><ul><li>Chronic Open Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Acute Closed Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Secondary Glaucoma</li><li>Normal-Tension Glaucoma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma laser devices market categorizes into several types based on the condition severity and type. Chronic open-angle glaucoma, the most common type, requires lasers for intraocular pressure reduction. Acute closed-angle glaucoma is a medical emergency demanding immediate laser intervention. Secondary glaucoma arises from other eye conditions or medications and may need customized laser treatments. Normal-tension glaucoma, characterized by optic nerve damage at normal pressure, utilizes lasers to manage progression. Each type necessitates tailored laser solutions to optimize patient outcomes.</p></p>
