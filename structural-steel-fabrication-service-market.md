<p><p>The structural steel fabrication service market includes various types tailored for specific construction needs. I-Beams, characterized by their 'I' shape, provide excellent load-bearing capabilities. L-Shaped angles offer versatility in architectural designs. Hollow Structural Sections (HSS) are favored for their strength-to-weight ratio and aesthetic appeal. Z-Shapes are often used for bracing and support, while T-Shapes are effective for connecting parts in truss configurations. Each type serves distinct structural applications in construction and engineering projects.</p></p>
