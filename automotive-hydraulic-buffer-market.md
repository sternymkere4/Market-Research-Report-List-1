<p><ul><li>Single-Tube</li><li>Double Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Hydraulic Buffer Market is primarily segmented into two types: Single-Tube and Double-Tube buffers. Single-Tube buffers consist of a single cylinder, providing a simpler design and lightweight construction, often preferred in smaller vehicles for basic damping functions. In contrast, Double-Tube buffers feature an inner and outer tube, allowing for enhanced performance, better heat dissipation, and improved handling characteristics. This makes Double-Tube buffers suitable for higher-end vehicles and applications requiring superior stability and comfort during dynamic driving conditions.</p></p>
