<p><p>The Resettable PTC fuses market is segmented into two primary types: polymer and ceramic. Polymer type PTCs utilize conductive polymers that change resistance with temperature, providing effective overcurrent protection while allowing for easy reset. In contrast, ceramic type PTCs are based on ceramic materials, offering higher temperature stability and robustness. Both types serve crucial roles in protecting electronic devices and circuits from overcurrent, with applications spanning consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, ensuring reliability and safety in various environments.</p></p>
