<p><p>The temperature screening system market can be categorized into two main types: mass screening and precise screening. Mass screening involves the rapid assessment of large groups, often using thermal cameras or infrared sensors to identify individuals with elevated temperatures efficiently, making it suitable for public spaces like airports and venues. In contrast, precise screening focuses on detailed assessments of individuals, typically using handheld devices for more accurate temperature readings, ideally suited for healthcare settings or entry points requiring higher scrutiny.</p></p>
