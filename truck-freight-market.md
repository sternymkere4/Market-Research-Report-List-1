<p><p>The truck freight market comprises various segments, including Less Than Truckload (LTL), Partial Truckload (PTL), and Full Truckload (FTL). LTL involves transporting smaller shipments that do not fill an entire truck, allowing for cost-effective sharing among multiple shippers. PTL caters to shipments that are larger than LTL but smaller than a full truck, optimizing space and cost. FTL involves large shipments that occupy the entire truck, ensuring direct delivery. Other market types may include expedited, intermodal, and refrigerated trucking services.</p></p>
