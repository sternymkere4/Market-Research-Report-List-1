<p><p>The conventional centrifugal chiller market is primarily segmented into single compressor and dual compressor types. In contrast, dual compressor chillers incorporate two compressors, enabling enhanced efficiency and flexibility, particularly in larger installations. This design allows for better load management, redundancy, and improved energy performance, appealing to commercial and industrial sectors with varying cooling demands.</p></p>
