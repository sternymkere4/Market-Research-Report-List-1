<p><strong>The Automatic Powder Coating Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corona Powder Coating Gun</li><li>Friction Powder Coating Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic powder coating gun market includes two primary types: corona powder coating guns and friction powder coating guns. Corona powder coating guns utilize an electrostatic charge to attract powder particles to a grounded surface, ensuring even coverage and reduced waste. Friction powder coating guns, on the other hand, rely on mechanical friction to create an electric charge, allowing for efficient powder transfer. Both types offer advantages in terms of speed, efficiency, and finish quality, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
