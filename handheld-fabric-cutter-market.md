<p><p>The handheld fabric cutter market includes two main types: rotary cutters and electric scissors. Rotary cutters feature a circular blade, allowing for precise, clean cuts through multiple layers of fabric, making them ideal for quilting and sewing projects. Electric scissors, on the other hand, utilize powered blades to effortlessly cut through various materials, reducing hand fatigue and enhancing cutting speed for larger tasks. Both tools offer innovative solutions for fabric cutting, catering to different user preferences and project requirements.</p></p>
