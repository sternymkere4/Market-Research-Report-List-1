<p><p>The wireless charger market comprises three main types: Magnetic Resonance, Electromagnetic Induction, and Radio Frequency. Magnetic Resonance utilizes resonant electromagnetic fields to transfer power over short distances, enabling multiple devices to charge simultaneously. Electromagnetic Induction relies on nearby coils to create a magnetic field that transfers energy, primarily used in smartphones and wearables. Radio Frequency technology employs electromagnetic waves to provide charging over longer distances, facilitating charging without the need for direct contact, enhancing convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
