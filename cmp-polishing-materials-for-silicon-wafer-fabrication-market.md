<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) polishing materials are essential in silicon wafer fabrication, primarily consisting of CMP slurries and CMP pads. CMP slurries are liquid suspensions containing abrasive particles and chemical agents that facilitate the planarization of wafer surfaces by removing material effectively. CMP pads, typically made from polyurethane or other materials, provide a controlled surface for the polishing process, ensuring uniform material removal. Together, these components enable high-precision manufacturing critical for semiconductor device production.</p></p>
