<p><p>The MEMS gyroscopes market is categorized into two primary types: sensing gyroscopes and indicating gyroscopes. Sensing gyroscopes are primarily used for measurement applications, detecting angular velocity and aiding in stabilization for devices like smartphones, drones, and automotive systems. Indicating gyroscopes, on the other hand, provide orientation information, commonly utilized in navigation systems and aircraft to maintain directional accuracy. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and functionality of various consumer electronics and industrial applications.</p></p>
