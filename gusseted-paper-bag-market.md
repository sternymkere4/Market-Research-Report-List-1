<p><p>Gusseted paper bags come in two primary types: side gusseted and bottom gusseted. Side gusseted bags have expandable sides that allow for greater volume and stability, making them ideal for packaging items like snacks and bulk goods. Bottom gusseted bags, on the other hand, feature a fold at the bottom, providing a sturdy base, which is advantageous for heavier products. Both types enhance storage efficiency and are commonly used in retail, foodservice, and promotional applications.</p></p>
