<p><ul><li>Lithium Ion</li><li>Lead-Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor jump starter market is primarily divided into two types: lithium-ion and lead-acid jump starters. Lithium-ion jump starters are lightweight, compact, and provide high power output with fast charging capabilities, making them ideal for modern vehicles. Conversely, lead-acid jump starters are heavier and bulkier but often come at a lower initial cost. They are reliable for older vehicles but may have shorter lifespan and longer recharge times compared to their lithium-ion counterparts. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
