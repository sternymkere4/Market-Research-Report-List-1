<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Permanent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic separation equipment is categorized into two primary types: electromagnetic and permanent magnets. Electromagnetic separators use electric currents to generate magnetic fields, allowing for adjustable strength and effectiveness in processing various materials. They are often suited for dynamic operations requiring frequent changes in separation requirements. Permanent magnets, on the other hand, utilize fixed magnetic fields generated by permanent magnets, providing a cost-effective, energy-efficient solution for continuous separation processes. Each type serves distinct industrial applications based on specific separation needs.</p></p>
