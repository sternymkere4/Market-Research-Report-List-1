<p><p>Slab gate valves are a type of isolation valve that utilize a flat slab-like gate for sealing against the flow of fluids. These valves are commonly used in oil and gas, water and wastewater management, and various industrial applications due to their robust design and ability to handle high pressures. They offer advantages such as low pressure drop, minimal turbulence, and reliable sealing capabilities, making them ideal for on-off and throttling applications.</p><p>The Slab Gate Valves Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
