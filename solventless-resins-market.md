<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Components</li><li>Three Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solventless resins market is categorized into single component, two component, and three component systems. Single component resins are ready to use and cure upon exposure to heat or moisture. Two component resins require mixing a resin and hardener for curing, allowing for tailored properties. Three component systems add a third element, often a modifier or filler, enhancing performance characteristics such as flexibility or adhesion. Each type offers distinct advantages suited for various applications in industries like construction and automotive.</p></p>
