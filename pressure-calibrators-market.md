<p><p>The pressure calibrators market is divided into two main types: benchtop pressure calibrators and portable pressure calibrators. In contrast, portable pressure calibrators are designed for field use, allowing technicians to calibrate instruments on-site. These devices are typically lightweight and battery-operated, making them ideal for various industrial applications where mobility and convenience are essential.</p></p>
