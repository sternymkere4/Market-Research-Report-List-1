<p><p>The ABS thermostatic radiator valve market primarily includes two types: self-operated temperature control valves and electric temperature control valves. Self-operated temperature control valves function through a mechanical system that adjusts radiator flow based on room temperature, ensuring efficient heating without external power. Conversely, electric temperature control valves utilize electronic sensors and actuators to provide precise temperature regulation and can be integrated with smart home systems. Both types enhance energy efficiency and comfort in residential and commercial heating applications.</p></p>
