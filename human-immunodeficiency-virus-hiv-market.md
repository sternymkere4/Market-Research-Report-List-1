<p><p>Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) is a virus that attacks the immune system, specifically targeting CD4 cells, which are crucial for a healthy immune response. If left untreated, HIV can lead to Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome (AIDS), a condition characterized by a severely weakened immune system, making individuals more susceptible to various infections and diseases. </p><p>The HIV market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing global awareness, advancements in antiretroviral therapies, and improved diagnostic techniques. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period, driven by rising prevalence rates, particularly in developing regions. Additionally, the expansion of government initiatives and funding towards HIV prevention, screening, and treatment programs is helping to bolster market growth. </p><p>Emerging trends include the development of long-acting injectable therapies and pre-exposure prophylaxis (PrEP) options, which are enhancing treatment adherence and preventing transmission. Technological advancements in diagnostics, such as rapid testing and home-based testing kits, are also transforming the landscape of HIV management. As new therapies and innovations emerge, the HIV market is poised for continued expansion, emphasizing the importance of ongoing research and development.</p></p>
