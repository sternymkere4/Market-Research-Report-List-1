<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spraybooths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross Flow Paint</li><li>Down Draft Paint</li><li>Side Down Draft Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spraybooths are essential in automotive and industrial painting, categorized mainly into three types. Cross Flow Paint Spraybooths facilitate airflow across the booth, helping to quickly clear overspray but may have limited containment. Down Draft Paint Spraybooths provide airflow from the ceiling to the floor, offering superior dust control and an even coating. Side Down Draft Paint Spraybooths combine features of both, allowing air to flow in from the sides and downwards, optimizing painting efficiency and minimizing contaminants. These designs enhance paint application quality and efficiency.</p></p>
