<p><p>The boat bow roller market can be categorized into two main types: roller and pivoting rollers. Roller models are fixed structures that provide stable support for the anchor, allowing smooth deployment and retrieval. Pivoting rollers, on the other hand, have a flexible design that enables the roller to tilt, accommodating various anchor types and providing better alignment during anchoring. Both types serve essential functions in enhancing anchoring efficiency and protecting the boat’s bow from damage, catering to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
