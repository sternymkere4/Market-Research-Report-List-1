<p><strong>The Chemical Foam Blowing Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endothermic</li><li>Exothermic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical foam blowing agents are categorized into two primary types: endothermic and exothermic agents. Endothermic agents absorb heat during the foaming process, resulting in lower temperatures and enhancing stability, making them suitable for applications requiring controlled expansion. In contrast, exothermic agents release heat, promoting rapid foam generation and often creating denser structures. The choice between these types depends on the desired properties of the final foam product, such as insulation performance, density, and processing conditions.</p></p>
