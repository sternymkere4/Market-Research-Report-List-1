<p><strong>The Light Automotive Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light automotive steering systems encompass three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which utilizes hydraulic fluid to enhance steering effort; Electronic Power Steering (EPS), that employs electric motors for smoother and more efficient steering control; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines hydraulic assistance with electronic control for optimized performance and energy efficiency. Each system offers unique advantages, with EPS becoming increasingly popular due to its lighter weight, energy savings, and integration with advanced driver-assistance technologies.</p></p>
