<p><p>The neurostimulation and neuromodulation device market is segmented into two primary types: internal and external devices. Internal devices are implanted within the body and provide targeted stimulation directly to the nervous system, often used for chronic pain management and neurological disorders. Both types aim to alter neural activity and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
