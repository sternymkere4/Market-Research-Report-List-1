<p><p>The color-coated steel market includes various types of coatings that enhance durability and aesthetics. PE (Polyester) coated steel offers good protection and is cost-effective, ideal for residential applications. HDP (High-Density Polyester) coated steel provides higher durability and UV resistance, suitable for commercial uses. SMP (Silicone Modified Polyester) coated steel combines flexibility and weather resistance, making it versatile for diverse applications. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) coated steel offers exceptional durability and chemical resistance, often used in high-end architectural projects.</p></p>
