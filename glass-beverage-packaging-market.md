<p><p>The glass beverage packaging market is divided into two main types: colorless glass and colored glass. Colorless glass, often referred to as flint glass, is favored for its clarity, allowing consumers to see the product inside, enhancing its appeal. In contrast, colored glass, available in shades like green and brown, is utilized for its ability to protect contents from light exposure, preserving flavor and quality. Both types cater to various beverage sectors, including wine, beer, and soft drinks.</p></p>
