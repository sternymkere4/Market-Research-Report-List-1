<p><p>The stretch films packaging market includes several key types, each with distinct properties. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) offers excellent stretchability and puncture resistance, making it ideal for securing loads. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is known for its flexibility and clarity. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) provides good cling and clarity, suitable for retail packaging. Polypropylene (PP) is known for its strength and resistance to moisture, often used in food packaging. Other materials may include biodegradable options and specialty films catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
