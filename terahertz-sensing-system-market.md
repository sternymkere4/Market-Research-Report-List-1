<p><p>The Terahertz Sensing System market is divided into two main types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing utilizes light in the terahertz frequency range to detect and characterize materials, making it ideal for high-resolution imaging and spectroscopy applications. In contrast, electronic sensing employs terahertz radiation through electronic devices to achieve data transmission and environmental monitoring, often offering faster response times. Both types serve diverse industries, including telecommunications, security, and healthcare, driving advancements in terahertz technology.</p></p>
