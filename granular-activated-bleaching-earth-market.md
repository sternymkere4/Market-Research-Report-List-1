<p><p>Granular Activated Bleaching Earth (GABE) market features various technologies for oil purification. Wet technology involves using a slurry of bleaching earth with oils, facilitating efficient adsorbent action while minimizing dust. Dry technology employs a powder form directly mixed with oils, enabling rapid adsorption but potentially creating dust issues. Vapour-phase technology uses vaporized solvents to enhance bleaching efficiency, promoting better oil quality. Other methods may combine these approaches or utilize innovative techniques for specific oil treatment needs, ensuring diversified solutions in the GABE market.</p></p>
