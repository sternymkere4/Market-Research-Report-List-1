<p><p>The Tubeless Insulin Patch Pump System market is segmented into two main types: fully-disposable and semi-disposable pumps. Fully-disposable systems are designed for single use and are discarded after the insulin reservoir is empty, offering convenience and reduced maintenance. In contrast, semi-disposable pumps allow for the reuse of certain components, such as the pump body, while only replacing the insulin reservoir and adhesive patches. This approach can be more cost-effective and environmentally friendly, catering to varying user preferences and needs.</p></p>
