<p><p>The medical display screen protectors market is categorized into three main types: PET, TPU, and other materials. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) protectors are known for their clarity and scratch resistance, making them ideal for high-resolution medical displays. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) offers superior flexibility and durability, providing better impact resistance and protection against chemicals. Other materials may include glass or specialized coatings, catering to specific requirements within medical environments, ensuring the screens remain intact, easy to clean, and maintain visual quality.</p></p>
