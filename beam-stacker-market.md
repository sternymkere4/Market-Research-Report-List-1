<p><p>The Beam Stacker market primarily features two types: Single Beam Storage and Double Beam Storage. Single Beam Storage systems utilize a single horizontal beam to support and organize goods, offering simplicity and space efficiency, ideal for smaller warehouses. In contrast, Double Beam Storage systems employ two beams, allowing for increased load capacity and better stability, making them suitable for larger operations with heavier inventories. Both systems optimize space and enhance operational efficiency in material handling and storage solutions.</p></p>
